About Us

Professional and affordable, Weddings On Film is a wedding videography company based in the heart of Surrey founded by professional film-maker Ivan Berry.

We use the very best equipment and technology to create stylish, cinematic wedding films which truly capture the love, laughter and tears of the most important day of your life for you to treasure forever.

Ivan has over 15 years working in television and production for companies such as Channel 5, Virgin Media and Disney Channel.
