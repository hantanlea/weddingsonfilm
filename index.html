About Us

Professional and affordable, Weddings On Film is a wedding production company based in the heart of Surrey, founded by professional videographer Ivan Berry, that specialises in bringing your special day to life.

Weddings on film uses the very best equipment and technology to capture the love, laughter and tears of the most important day of you life for you to treasure forever.

Ivan has had over 15 years working in television and production for companies such as Channel 5, Virgin Media and Disney Channel.
